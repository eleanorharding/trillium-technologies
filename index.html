How We Work

We are a technology company which develops innovative technologies and services in the areas of where we believe to be the fundamental Grand Challanges of this era: Ocean, Climate, and Space.

These Grand Challenges include space exploration and colonization, climate change monitoring and mitigation, as well as ocean exploration, colonization, and conservation. Trillium is a "mission oriented" company, meaning that everything that we build is built with a specific Mission objective in mind.

Key to Trillium's approach in tackling these challenges is it's own expertise in System Design and Systems Intelligence as well it's strong partnership network.
